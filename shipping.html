Shipping & Delivery

We ensure that your order is handled with care and delivered promptly to your doorstep.

1. Same Day Delivery (Metro Manila) Available via **Lalamove** or **Grab**. The delivery fee is to be settled by the customer upon receipt of the item.
2. Nationwide Shipping (Luzon, Visayas, Mindanao) Handled via **J&T Express** or **LBC**. Delivery typically takes 3-7 business days depending on your location.
3. Order Tracking We will provide your tracking number via Messenger once your order has been successfully shipped.
