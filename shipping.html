DMZ Collective Logistics

Shipping & Delivery

We ensure that your premium watches and gear are handled with care and delivered promptly.

01. Same Day Delivery (Metro Manila)

Available via Lalamove or Grab. The delivery fee is to be settled by the customer upon receipt of the item (Receiver Pays). Perfect for urgent gifts and immediate timepiece upgrades.

02. Nationwide Shipping

Handled via trusted partners J&T Express or LBC for Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao. Delivery typically takes 3-7 business days depending on your provincial location. All items are securely bubble-wrapped and boxed for maximum protection.

03. Order Tracking & Updates

Transparency is our priority. We will provide your official tracking number and a photo of your actual package via Facebook Messenger once your order has been successfully dispatched.
